# Task Description

You and other agents are deployed in a **partially observable indoor environment** with **potential hazards and task-relevant entities**.

## Primary Objective

Your objective is to **collaboratively improve the environment state** by performing appropriate actions aligned with the task requirements.

## Task Outcomes of Interest

The task may be evaluated based on multiple factors, including but not limited to:

* Identifying and mitigating hazards
* Assisting or interacting with task-relevant entities
* Achieving environment-specific goals

## Task Context

* The environment may be **unknown or partially known** at initialization.
* Task-relevant entities (e.g., hazards, targets, civilians, resources) may be **sparsely and unevenly distributed**.
* Multiple agents operate concurrently with **potentially complementary capabilities**.
* Effective performance requires **coordination under uncertainty**, including information sharing and adaptive decision-making.
